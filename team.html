Our Journey

Founded in 2010 by Jim Copper, Waffle World began in a small garage. Jim was frustrated with existing waffle makers - they were either too expensive or poor quality.

He teamed up with Jim copper, an engineer friend, to create a better waffle maker. Their first prototype was a hit with friends and family.

Today, we're proud to have helped over 50,000 customers make perfect waffles at home. We're still a small team, but we're mighty!
